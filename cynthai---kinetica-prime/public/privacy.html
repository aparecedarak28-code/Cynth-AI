Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Cynth AI ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Kinetica Prime Industries, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our proprietary Neural Synthesis platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Cynth AI, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: If you sign in via Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture for identity verification.
  • Neural Synthesis Data: We store the prompts you send and the responses synthesized by our proprietary neural matrix to provide chat history and personalized learning.
  • Usage Data: We may collect technical data such as IP addresses and browser types for security and performance monitoring of our neural nodes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Cynth AI neural synthesis service.
  • Personalize your experience and remember your synthesis history.
  • Improve our proprietary neural models and user interface.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our autonomous platform.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using advanced encryption. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access to our neural processing nodes.

4. Neural Matrix Privacy

Cynth AI utilizes a proprietary Neural Matrix to process your requests. Data is transmitted via secure, encrypted channels to our independent neural processing nodes. Your prompts are processed with absolute privacy and are not used to train external public models or shared with third-party AI providers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To request data deletion, please contact us at support@kineticaprime.com.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Kinetica Prime Industries.
