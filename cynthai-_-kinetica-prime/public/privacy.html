Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Cynth AI ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Kinetica Prime Industries, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI assistant.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Cynth AI, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: If you sign in via Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Chat Data: We store the prompts you send and the responses generated by the AI to provide chat history features.
  • Usage Data: We may collect technical data such as IP addresses and browser types for security and performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Cynth AI service.
  • Personalize your experience and remember your chat history.
  • Improve our AI models and user interface.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (a Google Cloud service). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

Cynth AI uses the Google Gemini API to process your requests. Your prompts are sent to Google's servers for processing but are not used to train their public models unless you explicitly opt-in via Google's own settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To request data deletion, please contact us at support@kineticaprime.com.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Kinetica Prime Industries.
